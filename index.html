About ImmunoGPT

ImmunoGPT is a suite of specialized AI models designed to accelerate discovery in immunology, including TCR-antigen binding prediction, vaccine design, immunotherapeutic optimization, and literature synthesis.

  • ✅ Fine-tuned on immune datasets like IEDB, TCIA, VDJdb
  • ✅ Modular: Core | TCR | Vax | Mol
  • ✅ Built with immunologists, for immunologists

Tested at Yale School of Medicine

ImmunoGPT's early-stage testing was conducted at Yale School of Medicine through collaborations with neuroscience and immunopathology researchers. The models were used to analyze immune-protein interactions and generate protocol drafts, yielding results comparable to state-of-the-art tools.
